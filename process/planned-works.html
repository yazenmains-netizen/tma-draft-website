Step by Step

The Planned Works Journey

Seven stages — each one documented, each one accountable.

01

Initial Enquiry & Scoping

We engage with your team to understand property volumes, access constraints, and any safeguarding considerations before committing to scope.

02

Survey & Assessment

Property-by-property inspections to record condition data, confirm scope, and flag any vulnerability or access considerations before programming begins.

03

Planning & Pre-Start

RAMS, works programmes, access schedules, and tenant notification plans are all issued and approved before a single operative is mobilised.

04

Delivery of Works

Directly employed, DBS-checked operatives work to agreed method statements. No subcontractors — full accountability at every level.

05

Quality Assurance

In-house QA inspections at property level — photographic evidence, compliance certificates, and snagging resolved before client sign-off is requested.

06

Handover & Documentation

A full works pack — certificates, warranties, as-built records, and per-property sign-off reports — issued to satisfy client asset management requirements.

07

Aftercare & Support

12-month workmanship guarantee with a dedicated aftercare contact. Any post-completion issues resolved at no cost within the guarantee period.
