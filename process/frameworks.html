Step by Step

The Framework Journey

Six clear stages — from appointment to continuous improvement.

01 — Appointment

Onboarding & Setup

Dedicated contract team introduced, protocols agreed, and all compliance documentation submitted before day one.

02 — Alignment

KPIs & Requirements Mapped

Contractual KPIs, SLAs, social value commitments, and resident engagement obligations built into our operations from day one.

03 — Planning

Programme & Resource Locked

Annual programmes agreed with the client before each delivery period — with contingency allowances and early-warning protocols in place.

04 — Delivery

Works On Programme

Directly employed operatives working to agreed method statements. Contract manager maintains regular client contact throughout delivery.

05 — Monitoring

Monthly KPI Reporting

Monthly dashboards covering completion rates, SLA performance, and resident satisfaction scores — plus quarterly reviews with senior stakeholders.

06 — Review

Audit & Continuous Improvement

In-house QA audits and a formal annual review — improvement plans, innovation proposals, and social value updates at every milestone.
