About TMA

Trusted Across the Midlands For Over 15 Years

TMA Property Maintenance Ltd is a family-run contractor with over 30 years of industry experience, working exclusively on contract for housing associations, local authorities, and large property groups.

Based in South Staffordshire on the West Midlands border, our directly employed and DBS-checked teams cover the Midlands and surrounding areas — delivering every trade from a single, accountable point of contact.

  • Approved CHAS, BM TRADA, Constructionline & NFRC supplier
  • NAPIT-registered electricians & Gas Safe engineers
  • Directly employed, DBS-checked operatives — no subcontractors
  • Works delivered across social housing and local authority contracts
  • 12-month workmanship guarantee on all works
