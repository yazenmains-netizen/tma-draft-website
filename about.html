About TMA Property Maintenance

A family-run contractor built on trust, accreditation, and over 15 years delivering for social housing and local authorities.

Our Story

Building the Future With Every Fix

TMA Property Maintenance Ltd is a family-run contractor with over 30 years of industry experience, working exclusively on contract for housing associations, local authorities, and large property organisations.

We are based in South Staffordshire on the West Midlands border. We currently cover work throughout the Midlands and surrounding areas, with contracts across some of the UK's most recognised social housing providers.

We are an approved CHAS, BM TRADA, Constructionline, and NFRC accredited supplier, with NAPIT-registered electricians and Gas Safe engineers across our teams.

We have operatives and teams available with vast experience within all aspects of construction and social housing, from basic plumbing all the way up to roofing and everything in between.

  • Family-run with 30+ years combined industry experience
  • Based in South Staffordshire, covering the Midlands and beyond
  • Directly employed, DBS-checked operatives — no subcontractors
  • Approved CHAS, BM TRADA, Constructionline & NFRC supplier
  • Contracted with leading housing associations and local authorities
  • 12-month workmanship guarantee on all works
